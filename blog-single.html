Understanding the Benefits of DTSCA Membership

March 15, 2024 Admin 5 Comments Featured

Discover how being a member of DTSCA can help you achieve your financial goals through our comprehensive savings and loan programs.

Why Choose DTSCA?

At DTSCA, we understand the unique financial needs of university staff members. Our association was established with the primary goal of providing accessible and affordable financial services to the Dilla University community.

1. Competitive Savings Programs

Our savings programs offer:

  • High interest rates (up to 7.9% annually)
  • Automatic salary deductions
  • Flexible withdrawal options
  • Regular dividend payments

2. Accessible Loan Services

Members can benefit from:

  • Low-interest loans (starting at 3% monthly)
  • Quick approval process
  • Flexible repayment terms
  • No hidden fees

"DTSCA has been instrumental in helping me achieve my financial goals. The savings program and loan facilities have made it possible for me to invest in my future."

Dr. Abebe Kebede, Associate Professor

3. Additional Member Benefits

Our members also enjoy:

  • Financial advisory services
  • Investment opportunities
  • Educational workshops
  • Emergency loan facilities
