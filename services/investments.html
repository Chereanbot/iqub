Frequently Asked Questions

The minimum investment varies by product: 10,000 ETB for Fixed Deposits, 5,000 ETB for Mutual Funds, and 50,000 ETB for the Real Estate Fund.

Returns are calculated based on the investment type and duration. Fixed Deposits earn monthly interest, while Mutual Funds and Real Estate investments may provide quarterly or annual returns.

Early withdrawals are possible but may incur penalties depending on the investment type and duration. Fixed Deposits have specific lock-in periods, while Mutual Funds offer more flexibility.
