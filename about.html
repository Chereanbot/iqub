Our Story

Since our establishment, DTSCA has been dedicated to serving the financial needs of Dilla University's academic and administrative staff.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable financial services to our members through efficient savings mobilization and credit provision, contributing to their economic empowerment and financial security.

Our Vision

To be the leading savings and credit association in Ethiopia's educational sector, known for excellence in member service, financial stability, and innovative solutions.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Operating with honesty and transparency in all our dealings.

Member Focus

Prioritizing our members' financial well-being and satisfaction.

Security

Ensuring the safety and security of our members' funds.

Growth

Committed to sustainable growth and development.
