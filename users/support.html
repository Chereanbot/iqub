Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for membership, you need to be a staff member of Dilla University. Visit our office with your staff ID, complete the membership form, and make your initial deposit. Our team will guide you through the process.

Loan requirements include: minimum 6 months membership, regular savings history, and good standing. The loan amount depends on your savings balance and salary. Contact our loan officer for detailed information.

You can check your account balance through our online portal, mobile app, or by visiting our office. For online access, log in to your account and view your dashboard for real-time balance information.

Our savings interest rates are competitive and calculated annually. Current rates vary based on the type of savings account and balance maintained. Contact our office for the latest interest rates.
