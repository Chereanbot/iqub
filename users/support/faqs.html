How do I apply for a loan?

To apply for a loan, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to the Loans section
  3. Click on "Apply for Loan"
  4. Fill out the application form
  5. Submit required documents

What documents are required for a loan application?

Required documents typically include:

  • Valid ID
  • Proof of income (last 3 months)
  • Bank statements
  • Proof of address

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email
  3. Follow the instructions in the reset email
  4. Create a new secure password
